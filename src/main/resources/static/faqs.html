Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I get Travel Companion?

    Go to Find Trip in the menu and enter Travelling from and travelling to airport location codes and click search button. You will see any matching trips listed. Click on Info link to know contact details. If you dont see any trips for your search, Add your travel plan so that someone can find and will reach you.

  • How can I add my travel plan?

    Go to Add Trip in the menu and add the necessary details. Click Add button and you will see popup message if your trip is saved successfully. If your trip is already added or duplicate, add tentative date for your trip.

  • How can I delete my saved trip?

    Once you login, you will see all your trips on home page. Click on the Trash icon to delete the specific trip

  • Is it safe to login with Google?

    Yes, it is. This website uses Google OAuth sign in to let other travellers know who you are. On clicking Google sign in button, you are being redirected to Googles website to enter you email and password. On successful authentication with google, we get basic details about you including your name, emailId from google. For more details on data please check privacy

  • How long my trips will be listed?

    Your trips will be listed as long as you do not delete or if it is one month older, whichever comes first

  • How can I communicate if I find a matching trip companion?

    Go to Find Trip in the menu and enter Travelling from and travelling to airport location codes and click search button. You will see any matching trips listed. Click on Info link to know contact details.You can choose the contact method preferred by the traveller

  • I need help with other issues. How do I report or contact?

    We understand you! Please contact us here. Contact Copayana Team
